Videography with Video Editing Course Overview

Welcome to the Videography with Video Editing course at Info-Tech Computer Training Center! This course is perfect for those who want to learn the art of capturing compelling videos and transforming them into professional-quality productions. Whether youâ€™re a beginner or looking to enhance your skills, this course covers everything you need to know about videography and video editing.

Course Content

  • Basics of Videography Start with the fundamentals of videography. Learn about different types of cameras, their settings, and how to use them to capture high-quality videos. Understand the principles of video production and the importance of storytelling in videography.
  • Camera Settings and Techniques: Master various camera techniques and settings, including focus, exposure, white balance, and frame rates. Learn how to use manual settings to have complete control over your video recordings.
  • Lighting and Sound Explore the critical aspects of lighting and sound in videography. Learn how to use natural and artificial lighting to enhance your videos. Understand how to capture clear and high-quality audio, and the importance of good sound design.
  • Composition and Framing Discover how to compose and frame your shots effectively. Study different types of shots and camera angles, and learn how to use them to tell a compelling story. Understand the rule of thirds and other composition techniques to create visually appealing videos.
  • Motion and Stabilization Learn how to capture smooth and stable video footage. Explore different methods of camera stabilization, including tripods, gimbals, and handheld techniques. Understand the importance of camera movement and how to execute various types of camera shots.
  • Introduction to Video Editing Get introduced to video editing software and tools. Learn how to import, organize, and manage your video footage in editing programs like Adobe Premiere Pro and Final Cut Pro.
  • Basic Editing Techniques: Start with basic editing techniques, including cropping, adjusting exposure, contrast, and color balance. Learn how to enhance your photos and correct common issues.
  • Advanced Video Editing Dive into advanced video editing techniques. Discover how to use special effects, motion graphics, and advanced color grading to enhance your videos. Learn how to synchronize audio and video, and how to use advanced editing tools for professional results.
  • Creating a Video Portfolio Learn how to create a professional video portfolio. Choose your best work, organize it effectively, and present it in a way that showcases your skills and style. Understand the importance of a demo reel and how to create one.
