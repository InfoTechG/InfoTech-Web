Photography with Photo Editing Course Overview

Welcome to the Photography with Photo Editing course at Info-Tech Computer Training Center! This course is designed for aspiring photographers and digital artists who want to enhance their skills in capturing stunning images and editing them to perfection. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your techniques, this course offers comprehensive training in both photography and photo editing.

Course Content

  • Fundamentals of Photography: Learn the basics of photography, including understanding your camera, its settings, and how to use them effectively. Explore concepts like exposure, composition, lighting, and framing to take professional-quality photos.
  • Camera Settings and Techniques: Master various camera settings and techniques to capture the perfect shot. Learn about aperture, shutter speed, ISO, and how to use manual mode to have full control over your photography.
  • Lighting and Composition: Discover the importance of lighting in photography and how to use natural and artificial light to your advantage. Study composition techniques to create visually appealing and balanced photographs.
  • Portrait Photography: Focus on portrait photography and learn how to take stunning portraits. Understand how to work with subjects, pose them, and use lighting to highlight their features.
  • Landscape and Nature Photography: Explore landscape and nature photography. Learn how to capture the beauty of the natural world, including tips for shooting in various weather conditions and times of day.
  • Introduction to Photo Editing: Get introduced to photo editing software and tools. Learn how to import, organize, and manage your photos in editing programs like Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom.
  • Basic Editing Techniques: Start with basic editing techniques, including cropping, adjusting exposure, contrast, and color balance. Learn how to enhance your photos and correct common issues.
  • Advanced Photo Editing: Dive into advanced photo editing techniques. Discover how to retouch portraits, remove unwanted elements, and apply creative effects to transform your images.
  • Creating a Portfolio: Learn how to create a professional photography portfolio. Choose your best work, organize it effectively, and present it in a way that showcases your skills and style.
